IA Word Guess

How to Play (Click to Expand)
  • Guess the 5-letter secret word in 6 tries.
  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. Press Enter to submit.
  • After each guess, the tiles and keyboard keys will change color to show how close your guess was:
    • **Pumpkin Orange:** Letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
    • **Spooky Purple:** Letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
    • **Dark Gray:** Letter is not in the word at all.
